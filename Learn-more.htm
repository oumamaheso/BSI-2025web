About Us

Our company, BSI South Africa (BSI SA), specializes in delivering financial services and training solutions to individuals and businesses. We offer portfolio management, financial planning, and advisory services, as well as training programs in financial services, debt recovery, and business development.

Our Company is divided into three sections,College,Acounting and training solutions and Finex.

Internships

At BSI, we believe in the importance of investing in people. We recognize that our current success is built on the contributions of those who came before us. With this understanding, we select a group of interns each year to support and strengthen our team. We're proud to say that many of them have successfully entered the broader economy, with many still remaining active members of the BSI community.
